A lighthouse, or phare in French, is used to guide ships on their journey and to warn them of danger. At Phare Information Security, my goal is to guide those embarking on their information security journey and warn them of potential cyberthreats. I accomplish this primarily by maintaining an information security-focused blog for the benefit of any interested readers. I am also pleased to offer consulting services as my schedule allows.
